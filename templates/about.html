About Us

Copy-Cat is a sophisticated tool using state of the art computational linguistic algorithms to detect plagiarism. Copy-cat can be used in multiple ways.

  1. Manual operation - Enter a body of text and we will evaluate it for you
  2. File Upload - We accept docx, doc files
  3. API access - Integrate Copycat into your existing workflows using our API
  • Copywriting : Protect your original content
  • Education : Copy-cat is a great companion for educational assessment. Call out students who've been getting Google to do their homework
  • Business : Your content should always remain yours. Use copy-cat to find the copy-cats out there

How it works:

Our algorithms process your text and create a 'signature' of sorts. This is then scanned across a number of major Search engine API's in a highly efficient automated manner. We follow through on each result and process the pages to filter their content. Our local cache speeds up querytimes and helps us serve you in a more cost effective manner!
